SEBI (LODR) due date calculator

Select the financial year and quarter end to compute mandatory filing deadlines for listed entities under the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. Consolidated per the SEBI LODR Master Circular dated 30 January 2026.

For general reference only. Consult a qualified professional for advice specific to your entity's facts and circumstances. Event-based and ongoing compliances (material events, board meeting outcomes, record date intimation, trading window closure, etc.) run on trigger-based timelines and aren't covered here — see the full compliance calendar for those.

Company ROC fee & penalty calculator

Estimate MCA filing fees and late penalties for Private, Public, OPC and Small Companies, under the Companies (Registration Offices and Fees) Rules, 2014.

Indicative only — ad valorem fees for charge forms (CHG-1/CHG-9) and state-wise stamp duty (e.g. SH-7, SPICe+) are not covered here; verify exact figures on the MCA portal. The general filing fee slab (Item 5/6 of the Annexure, used for ADT-1, DIR-12, etc.) is the same ₹200–₹600 slab for every company type — OPC/Small Company status only affects the incorporation fee and certain government-application fees, not this routine filing fee, so the Company type selector above is for reference only and does not change the amount calculated. DIR-3 KYC is now due once every 3 financial years (30 June), anchored to each director's own DIN cycle rather than a fixed calendar date for everyone — confirm the director-specific due year on the MCA portal before relying on the toggle above. Filing DIR-3 KYC Web solely to update personal details attracts a flat ₹500 fee at any time, per the Companies (Registration Offices and Fees) Amendment Rules, 2026. For general reference only, not professional advice.

LLP fee & penalty calculator

Calculate statutory filing fees and slab-multiplier late penalties for Form 8 and Form 11, under the LLP (Second Amendment) Rules, 2022.

Indicative only, based on the LLP (Second Amendment) Rules, 2022. Does not account for stamp duty or professional fees. Verify on the MCA portal before filing.

MSME delayed payment interest calculator

Calculate indicative compound interest owed to Micro and Small Enterprises for delayed payments, under Section 16 of the MSMED Act, 2006 (3× RBI Bank Rate, with monthly rests).

Indicative only. Uses the bank rate entered above for the entire delay period and does not account for historical RBI rate changes. Agreed payment date is capped at 45 days from acceptance per Sec. 15; if left blank, the 15-day default (no written agreement) is used. Consult a professional or the MSME Samadhaan portal for a definitive figure.

E — Ongoing Compliances

Not tied to a fixed timeline, but require continuous monitoring throughout the year

  • Reg. 30 — Continuous material event disclosures as and when triggered
  • Reg. 46 — Maintenance and updation of the functional company website
  • Reg. 9 — Preservation of documents as per the policy framework
  • Reg. 3(5), PIT Regulations — Maintenance of the Structured Digital Database
  • Trading Window Closure — Under the Code of Conduct during UPSI periods
  • Reg. 7, PIT Regulations — Continual disclosures by promoters, promoter group, directors and designated persons, as applicable
  • Investor Grievance Redressal — Timely resolution through SCORES and other channels
  • Contact Details — Updating contact details of the Compliance Officer, RTA and Key Managerial Personnel on stock exchange portals and the company website

Insider trading-related items above fall under the SEBI (PIT) Regulations, 2015 rather than LODR, but are routinely tracked alongside LODR compliances by Company Secretaries. Unless otherwise stated, all regulations above are under the SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

Board resolution drafter

Standard-form board resolution language for common corporate actions. Listed-entity templates additionally track SEBI (LODR) obligations; Other covers private and unlisted public companies. Always tailor to your Articles, Board-approved policies and the specific facts before use.

AGM / EGM notice drafter

Listed-entity notices follow the fuller structure used by NSE/BSE-listed companies — e-voting, cut-off date, book closure and Registrar particulars included. Other covers a simpler notice for private and unlisted companies. Both the Ordinary Business and Special Business sections are drafted with common items by default for AGM and EGM alike — remove whichever items or section do not apply to your meeting. Always tailor to your Articles and SS-2 before issuing.

E-form field validator

Checks that common MCA / GST / Income Tax identifiers are in the correct format before you key them into an e-form. PAN, DIN, CIN, LLPIN, GSTIN and TAN have no free public API from anyone, government or paid provider — "Check on …" opens the correct official government portal for a quick manual lookup instead.
